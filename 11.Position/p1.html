Position

        values of position property
        1. relative (allows to handle only x axis(left) & y axis(top) )
        2. absolute (allows to handle all 4 sides (left, right, top, bottom))
        3. fixed (same as absolute but it differs only in scroll, it does not move the content even with scroll action)

        4 properties associated to position
        top - y axis
        left - x axis
        right
        bottom
